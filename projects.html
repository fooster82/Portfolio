Projects

Below are the projects I've worked on during the Futureproof course.

StreetHub - Repo

A website for people to anonymously leave notices for people in their local area to see, such as upcoming events or lost pet notices. Made with George Hickey and Chris Anstead.

Trabbit - Repo

A weekly habit tracker where users can login and register new habits and set a weekly goal for completion of each habit. Made with Golnar Kaviani, Olu Akano and Calum McDougall.

Kahtwoot - Repo

A mobile-first designed quiz app for groups of friends to team up and compete against others to answer as many questions correctly as possible to gain the most points. Best played on a phone or tablet! Made with Rachael Yonoff, Zach Brown and Max Mackie.

Nintino - Repo

A mobile-first designed casino-themed games app with checkers as a launch game. Best played on a phone or tablet! Made with Golnar Kaviani, George Hickey, James Pollington and Cameron Bromley.
